ABOUT

At Nova Assist, we're revolutionizing assistive technology to empower individuals with disabilities and redefine what's possible. From smart sticks with real-time object detection to robotic arms translating sign language, our products break down barriers to independence and mobility.

But our mission goes beyond products - it's about transforming lives. You can be a part of this transformation. Sign up for our newsletter to stay updated on the latest advancements, events, and exclusive offers. Consider supporting our mission by donating to Nova Assist. Your contributions help us continue our research, development, and outreach efforts.

Don't miss our informative webinars, where industry experts share insights on assistive technology and accessibility. Whether you're a caregiver, healthcare professional, or individual with a disability, our webinars provide valuable resources for learning and growth.

Join us as we push the boundaries of innovation and revolutionize the way individuals with disabilities experience the world. Together, we can create a future where possibilities are endless, and every life is enriched.
